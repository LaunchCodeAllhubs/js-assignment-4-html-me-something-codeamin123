Resume -> Professional Journey

Since High School Graduation

Graduated high school in >2007 without a clue on what I wanted to study or where I wanted to work.

Went to the Community College of Philadelphia for two years to try to figure out what and where I wanted to study. I chose to study Psychology and eventually knew I wanted to go to Temple University

I then decided to transfer to Millersville to live on campus and decide if I wanted to continue to study Psychology.

Millersville Mascot/Photo

After a year at Millersville, I decided to continue to study Psychology and attend Temple University back in Philadelphia.

Temple Mascot/Photo

I graduated from Temple University with a Bachelors of Art degree in 2012.

After Graduating, I worked in retail (CVS,jewelry store) until getting my driver's license and applying for a job as a Counselor for adults with Mental Health Disorders

Worked as a Counselor for around 1 year and 4 months until applying to my current job as a Parole/Probation Officer

During my time as a Parole/Probation Officer, I started to hear more about coding. I decided to buy a few books to try to learn what this field was all about. After learning a little bit of python, I decided that the logic needed to complete the assignments was very interesting.

I decided that I would like to pursue this as a full time career. I started to try to self-learn, but always gave up after I got to a part where it was difficult to continue

Learned about LaunchCode through social media and decided to give it a try.

As of today, I have worked as a Parole/Probation Officer for over 8 and a half years and I am currently in the first half of LaunchCode 101!
